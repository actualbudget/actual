Schedules and rules: avoid auto-generating a redundant payee `is`-rule when a `oneOf`-rule already maps the payee to the same category; monthly schedules starting on the 29th/30th/31st now fall back to the last day of months that don't have that day; and a scheduled transaction posted ahead of its due date is now correctly counted as paid for the current cycle.
